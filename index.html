How does the autonomous vehicle industry prove that their technology is safe?

By Emily Yip, Data Science at the University of California San Diego

Autonomous vehicles (AV) are transforming modern transportation. As AV companies expand into various metropolitan cities such as San Francisco, Las Vegas, and Phoenix, concerns regarding passenger safety continue to shape public perception. Understanding how autonomous car companies demonstrate that their vehicles are safe is critical to gaining public trust and successful widespread deployment. This document provides an overview of the methods and data that AV companies employ to communicate the safety of their vehicles.
